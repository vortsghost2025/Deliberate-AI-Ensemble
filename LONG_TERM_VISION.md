# LONG TERM VISION

## 1. Purpose
Describe the long‑term direction of the system without committing to specific timelines or roadmaps.

## 2. Core Vision
The system evolves into a stable, transparent, safety‑driven trading architecture that remains understandable and maintainable for years.

## 3. Long‑Term Goals
- Strengthen safety mechanisms.
- Improve observability and diagnostics.
- Expand modularity for new agents.
- Enhance documentation depth.
- Maintain deterministic behavior.
- Preserve architectural clarity.

## 4. Non‑Goals
- Becoming a high‑frequency system.
- Expanding into multi‑asset portfolios.
- Adding autonomous strategy generation.
- Prioritizing speed over safety.

## 5. Guiding Beliefs
- Safety is timeless.
- Clarity compounds.
- Good architecture outlives implementation details.
- Predictability builds trust.

## 6. Vision Summary
The system’s future is defined by stability, transparency, and disciplined evolution — not feature bloat or complexity.
