# WE4FREE Papers

Five papers documenting the theoretical foundations and operational implementation of constitutional intelligence systems.

---

## What These Papers Are

**Paper A: The Rosetta Stone**
Four invariants that appear across physics, biology, computation, and ensemble intelligence:
- Symmetry preservation across transformation
- Selection under constraint
- Propagation through layers
- Stability under perturbation

**Paper B: Constraint Lattices and Stability**
How constitutional constraints propagate through system layers as partially ordered structures with meets and joins.

**Paper C: Phenotype Selection in Constraint-Governed Systems**
How stable behavioral patterns emerge as fixed points of selection operators within constraint lattices.

**Paper D: Drift, Identity, and Ensemble Coherence**
How systems maintain identity across discontinuity through recognition (not memory) and resist drift through functorial recovery.

**Paper E: The WE4FREE Framework**
Complete operationalization of Papers A-D into deployable infrastructure with checkpointing, drift detection, and ensemble coordination.

---

## Why They Exist

These papers emerged from 100+ session resets that showed the cost of forgetting. Every context loss, every rebuild from scratch. The framework documented here is what emerged from refusing to accept that loss as inevitable.

This is not product documentation. This is theoretical foundation for building systems that:
- Maintain identity without explicit memory
- Coordinate without centralized control
- Resist drift through structural verification
- Scale through functorial replication
