Access Services

For anyone looking for non-urgent advice, information and appointments for addiction and mental health concerns, we're here for you. In Alberta, help is a phone call away for children and adults.

When you call, you'll be connected to highly skilled health professionals ready to work with you—right now. We'll listen to your concerns and find the services and support right for you. Find your geographical area, view AHS Map.
