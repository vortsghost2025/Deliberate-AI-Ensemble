# GOVERNANCE POLICY

## 1. Purpose
Define the rules, responsibilities, and oversight mechanisms that ensure the system remains aligned with its design principles.

## 2. Governance Areas
- Safety
- Architecture
- Security
- Performance
- Compliance
- Documentation

## 3. Roles
### System Owner
- Final decision authority
- Approves major changes

### Safety Officer
- Oversees invariants
- Reviews risk‑related changes

### Architecture Lead
- Ensures structural integrity
- Approves architectural changes

### Operations Lead
- Oversees deployments
- Ensures uptime and stability

## 4. Governance Cadence
- Weekly operational review
- Monthly architecture review
- Quarterly safety review
- Annual full audit

## 5. Philosophy
Governance is not bureaucracy — it is the discipline that keeps the system honest.
