Do you need to talk to someone right away?

Caseworkers are available to help you 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Many organizations offer free and confidential support services and professional consultations. Contact them:

  • Info-Social 811 (811, option 2) ΓÇô To quickly reach a professional in psychosocial intervention
  • Local community services centre (CLSC) ΓÇô To receive support and help if you have symptoms of mental disorder or a social problem
  • 1-866-APPELLE (1-866-277-3553) - text at 535353 - live chat ΓÇô To find support if you or a loved one is considering suicide
  • 911 ΓÇô In case of immediate danger to yourself or a loved one

Other help lines and telephone services are also available, consult their information.
